You may view or edit your Personal Information and preferences by visiting the Website and logging into your PizzaVegan Account (the “PizzaVegan Account”) online. From your PizzaVegan Account, you can choose to receive or stop the delivery of future newsletters, promotional e-mail, and surveys from PizzaVegan.

Please note that this does not apply to the display of online advertising, nor does this apply to the receipt of mandatory service communications that are considered part of the Services, which you may receive periodically unless you cancel your PizzaVegan Account.

Collection of Your Personal Information

When you open a user account with PizzaVegan, we ask you to provide certain Personal Information, such as your name, e-mail address, home or work address, and/or telephone number. We may also collect general demographic information, such as your age, gender, ZIP code, preferences, and interests. We may also collect information about your visit to the Website, such as pages viewed, links clicked and other similar navigation action in connection with the Website.

When you receive promotional e-mails or newsletters from PizzaVegan, we may use customized links or similar technologies to determine whether such newsletters or e-mail has been opened and which links you click. This information is used to provide you more focused e-mail communications and/or other information.

Personal Information that is Automatically Collected

In addition to Personal Information you provide to us, PizzaVegan may receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with our websites or services. PizzaVegan may additionally automatically collect information about your visit to our website, such as pages viewed, links clicked, and other similar navigation action in connection with the website. Most Web browsers accept cookies, and if your browser accepts cookies, PizzaVegan may automatically collect individualized information regarding your visits to the PizzaVegan website (“Individual Usage Information”) to provide you with a more personalized user experience. As part of this use of information, we may provide aggregate information to our partners about how our users, collectively, use our websites. If you do not wish to have your Individual Usage Information collected, please see the “Use of Cookies” section below for further information.

PizzaVegan use of cookies is only for more accurate traffic analysis. PizzaVegan cookies do not collect Personal Information.
